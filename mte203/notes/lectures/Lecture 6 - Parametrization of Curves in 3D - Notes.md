Parametrizing Curves
 To parametrize a curve formed by the intersection of surfaces
1. Draw a 3D sketch (a.k.a. graph) of the curve of intersection
2. After observing the surface equations, determine a parametric
equation for each variable and determine the parameter range
3. Check the direction of the curve (plot 3 points minimum)
4. If direction is not correct, determine new parametric equations
(usually a change of sign is a first good try)
 There exists infinite parametrizations for the same curve
With some more practice you will “master the art” of
parametrization
